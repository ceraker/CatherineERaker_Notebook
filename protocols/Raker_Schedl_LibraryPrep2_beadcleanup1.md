#### 2. 0.8 Bead Cleanup After 1st Amplification (PCR1)
##### Materials
- Freshly made 80% ethanol
- 10mM Tris HCl conical
- MagBio beads (4C fridge)
- Magnetic plate
- New set of strip tubes or plate
- Orbital shaker

##### Steps: ON POST-PCR BENCH
1. Take beads out 30+ minutes before starting the cleanup and allow them to reach room temperature
  - Can warm them in your hand
  - Swirl to re-distribute beads
2. Slowly pipet beads into basin/trough
  - Get more than you need and then replace the rest
3. See Step 1 for volumes of PCR1 product (likely 34.5 ul)
4. Calculate volume of beads that is 0.8x the volume of PCR1 product (likely 0.8 * 34.5 = 27.6 ul)
5. Add beads to first row of sample tubes using the multichannel
  - Pipet VERY SLOWLY
6. Pipet mix 10-20 times
  - Super slow: NO bubbles or droplets on the sides of the tubes!
  - CANNOT spin down
7. Repeat for each row of samples, changing tips every time
8. Shake on Orbital Shaker at 200 rpm for at least 15 minutes
9. *While shaking:* make fresh 80% ethanol
  - Fill up to 40 mL with 100% ethanol (on bench)
  - Fill rest of the way to 50 mL with molecular grade water
10. *While shaking:* take materials for 2nd PCR our of -20 if running that day
11. Put sample plate on magnet plate
12. Shake for 1-2 minutes or until beads pellet (liquid should be clear)
  - Remove plate from shaker but keep it on the magnet
13. Prepare a trough/basin for waste liquid
14. Pipet out clear liquid (everything you want to clean out: primers, etc)
  - 5-10 ul less than total volume (likely 57 ul)
  - Avoid accidentally picking up beads
  - Go down the side of the tube away from the bead pellet
  - Be efficient/fast: beads should NOT dry out
15. Repeat for each row of samples, always changing tips
16. Wash with 80% ethanol
  - Add 100 ul ethanol to opposite side from pellet (this is more than the original volume)
  - Let sit ~30 seconds
  - Remove ethanol
  - Repeat
  - Pipet out residual ethanol (use p20 set to 20ul)
17. Take plate off the magnet
18. Resuspend beads in 25 ul of 10mM Tris HCl pH 8.5 (always the same volume)
  - Wash beads off the sides of the tubes
  - Pipet mix: pipet up and down many times
  - Fast! Don't let the beads dry!
19. Repeat for each row of samples, changing tips every time
20. If there are droplets on the side of the tubes: tap them gently down or do a VERY BRIEF spin down in the minifuge (try to avoid this)
21. Shake plate on Orbital Shaker at 200 rpm for at least 5 minutes
22. *If running PCR2 that day, see create Master Mix: see Step 3 PCR2 Protocol*
23. *While shaking:* prepare and label new tubes/plate for the samples
24. Place plate back on magnet plate
25. Shake for 1-2 minutes or until beads pellet (liquid should be clear)
26. Remove 24 ul of supernatant and save in labeled tubes (no beads!)
27. If not running PCR2: save samples in -20
28. Waste trough can be left out to dry overnight and then thrown away in regular trash
