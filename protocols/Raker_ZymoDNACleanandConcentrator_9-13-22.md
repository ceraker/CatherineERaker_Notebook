## Kit: Zymo DNA Clean and Concentrator
Protocol adapted from the Zymo DNA Clean and Concentrator, and from previous clean-ups performed on 9/12/22.

#### DNA Lab Bench Prep
1. Wipe down workspace  with 10% bleach
2. Wipe down workspace  with Type II DI water
3. Wipe down workspace with 70% Ethanol
4. Workspace and equipment include: lab bench, tube racks being used, pipettes, vortexers, outside of centrifuges

#### Successful Volumes
- Starting DNA volume: 20ul
- DNA Binding Buffer volume: 40ul
- DNA Elution Buffer volume: 11ul

#### Protocol
1. Transfer desired volume of DNA to new 1.5 mL tube (20ul)
2. Add 2x volume of DNA Binding Buffer (40ul)
3. Vortex and spin down
4. Transfer total volume to a Zymo-Spin Column (60ul)
5. Place spin column in collection tube (can use tube provided or your own)
6. Centrifuge at 11000 rcf for 30 seconds
7. Discard flow through
8. Add 200ul DNA Wash Buffer to Column
9. Centrifuge at 11000 rcf for 30 seconds
10. Discard flow through
11. Add 200ul DNA Wash Buffer to Column
12. Centrifuge at 11000 rcf for 30 seconds
13. Transfer Column to final fully labeled 1.5 mL tube
14. Discard flow through from wash step
  - This keeps the flow through off the Column, and keeps it from contaminating the final sample
15. Add >/= 10ul DNA Elution Buffer directly to Column matrix (11ul)
16. Incubate at room temperature for 1 minute
17. Centrifuge at 11000 rcf for 30 seconds
18. Discard Column
19. Can perform quanitification steps or store samples in -20 for later
20. Clean-up workstation! 

#### Notes
1. All tubes used should be nuclease-free
2. Tubes used throughout the procedure do not require full labels, but final sample tubes should include sample ID, initials, and date
3. Remember to close things right after you finish using them!
